About us

Our Services

Comprehensive Health Monitoring

Elder Care is equipped with cutting-edge sensors that continuously monitor vital signs like heart rate, body temperature, and oxygen saturation. Whether you're at home or on the move, you can trust Elder Care to provide real-time data and instant alerts to caregivers, ensuring prompt response to any health irregularities.

Personalized Health Insights

Our fitness band collects health data to provide personalized insights and trends. With Elder Care, users and their families can monitor long-term health patterns, helping to make informed decisions about lifestyle changes, medications, or medical consultations.

Why Choose Elder Care?

  • Real-time Health Monitoring: Continuous tracking of vital signs ensures that you're always aware of your health status.
  • Advanced Movement Detection: Detects falls, seizures, and unusual body movements, sending instant alerts to caregivers.
  • AI-Powered Early Warnings: Predicts potential health risks based on your medical history and current data.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy to operate, even for seniors with limited tech experience.

Emergency Alerts

In case of any abnormal health conditions or accidents, Elder Care instantly sends emergency alerts to designated family members or caregivers through a mobile app. This feature ensures that help is always just a notification away.
